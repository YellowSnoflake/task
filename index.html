How this works
• Set your workday hours and minutes-per-task. Total tasks auto-calculate (you can override).
• Use Start/Pause to track elapsed time, or ignore the timer and enter elapsed manually by focusing the Elapsed field and typing (hh:mm:ss).
• Target time = tasks_done × minutes_per_task.
• Over/Under compares your actual elapsed vs. what you should have used based on how many tasks you’ve done.
• Progress ring animates as you add tasks; confetti fires at 100%. State is saved locally.
